Introducing Handan

A long history

Handan stands as the only Chinese city to retain its original name for over 3,000 years Its legacy spans from the Shang Dynasty's imperial retreat to the capital of the Zhao Sttate during the Warring States period. Located at the strategic Fukou Pass-a key gatewayy between the Taihang Mountains and the Norrth China Plain-the city has long been a regional hub of politics, commerce, and culture. Histtorical figures like Xunzi once walked its streets,and events like the "Hufu u Qishe" (adoption ofnomadic cavalry attire) unfolded here, shapingChinese civilizzation across millennia

Places of interest

Handan boasts a rich tapestry of cultural andnatural attractions, including:
● 2 National 5A-Level Scenic Areas
● Wa Palace in She County (2015) -a sacred site linked to the goddess Nüwa;
● Guangfu Ancient City (2017)-a 2,600-year-old "city on the water" renowned for its Tai Chi heritage;
● 19 National 4A-Level Sites, such as Xiangtang Mountain Grottoes, Zhao Royal Park, and the revolutionary 129th Division HeadquarterrsMemorial;
● Cultural landmarks like the Cizhou Kiln遗址 and the immersive "Dream Back to Handan"3D light show in Guangfu ,where history meets moderrn technology.

Idioms Capital

Recognized as the "Capital of Chinese Idioms," Handan is the birthplace of over 1,584 idioms and historical allusions. Classic tales like"完璧归赵"(Return the Jade Intact to Zhao)and"胡服骑射"(Adopting Nomadic Dress and cavalry Tactics) originated here.
The city brings idioms to life through:
● The Idiom-themed Bus Route 26, featuringidiom decorations and videos;
● An "Idiom Street" with lantern displayss and engraved manhole covers;
● The vibrant Handan Dao Historic Block, where visitors dress in traditional attire and stroll past sites like Learning-to-Walkk Bridge and Congtai Terrace.
